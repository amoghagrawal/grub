FAQ

Common questions about coffee chains.

Starbucks typically has the highest caffeine levels in their drip coffee, while Dunkin' has slightly lower caffeine content on average.

Dunkin' generally offers better value with lower prices than Starbucks, which focuses more on premium offerings and experience.

Both chains have ethical sourcing programs. Starbucks has C.A.F.E. Practices while Dunkin' aims for 100% responsible sourcing by 2025.
